Keyboard Shortcuts
MOVEMENT
W or ↑ Move Up
A or ← Move Left
S or ↓ Move Down
D or → Move Right
OTHERS
Space Reset
1 2 3 Select Object
ROTATION & SCALE
Q Rotate Left
E Rotate Right
R Scale Up
T Scale Down
MOUSE CONTROLS
Drag: Move object
Scroll Up: Scale up
Scroll Down: Scale down
